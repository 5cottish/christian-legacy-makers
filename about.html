Our Story

Christian Legacy Makers (CLM) is a faith-driven, community-focused organization founded on 4th October 2021 with a vision to restore hope, dignity, and opportunity to children and mothers across Uganda.

Inspired by Christian values of compassion, stewardship, and service, CLM seeks to build a generation rooted in literacy, empowered through practical skills, and strengthened spiritually through discipleship.

We believe that creating long-lasting generational change begins with supporting the most vulnerable‚Äîchildren and mothers‚Äîwho often face barriers in education, health, economic stability, and spiritual growth.

Operating primarily in Wakiso District, Uganda, we work through meaningful community partnerships and strategic interventions to create sustainable impact that shapes families and builds a legacy of faith for future generations.

Our Goals

Six strategic objectives guiding our work

To increase literacy levels among children and mothers in underserved communities.

02

To reduce poverty among Christian families by equipping mothers with income-generating skills.

03

To raise spiritually grounded children and families who actively live out Christian values.
