The LED Framework

Literacy, Empowerment, Discipleship — Holistic transformation for sustainable change

Everything CLM does revolves around our core strategic framework: LED. Through these three pillars, we address the educational, economic, and spiritual needs of children and mothers in underserved communities.

Literacy

Education & Reading Programs

Improving access to quality education, early childhood development, reading support, and academic opportunities for children and young mothers.

Key Initiatives:

  • Community Reading Clubs

    Weekly reading sessions in community centers fostering literacy skills and a love for books among children aged 5-15.

  • Early Childhood Development

    Pre-school preparation programs focusing on foundational literacy and numeracy skills for children aged 3-6.

  • School Support Program

    Providing scholastic materials, uniforms, and tuition support to vulnerable children to ensure school attendance and retention.

  • Adult Literacy Classes

    Basic reading and writing classes for mothers who missed formal education opportunities.

Program Impact

500+ Children enrolled in reading clubs
85% Improvement in reading levels
12 Community reading clubs active

Support Empowerment Programs

Equipping mothers and youth with practical skills, financial literacy, health knowledge, and entrepreneurship capacities to enable self-reliance and household stability.

Key Initiatives:

  • Vocational Skills Training

    Hands-on training in tailoring, crafts, baking, and agribusiness providing marketable skills for income generation.

  • Financial Literacy Workshops

    Training on budgeting, saving, and basic business management to ensure sustainable use of income.

  • Start-up Kit Distribution

    Providing graduates with tools and materials to start their own businesses (sewing machines, baking equipment, etc.).

  • Health & Nutrition Education

    Workshops on maternal health, child nutrition, and disease prevention for healthier families.

Discipleship

Spiritual Growth & Mentorship

Strengthening spiritual growth, Christian character, and community fellowship through teaching, mentorship, counseling, and faith-based leadership development.

Key Initiatives:

  • One-on-One Mentorship

    Pairing mature Christian women with young mothers for spiritual guidance and life skills coaching.

  • Bible Study Groups

    Weekly community Bible studies exploring Scripture and its application to daily challenges.

  • Children's Ministry

    Age-appropriate Bible lessons, character building activities, and worship for children.

  • Counseling Services

    Free Christian counseling for families dealing with trauma, marriage issues, and personal struggles.

  • Leadership Development

    Training emerging community leaders in servant leadership and church-based ministry skills.

Program Impact

300+ Active mentorship relationships
25 Bible study groups meeting weekly
50 Trained community leaders
